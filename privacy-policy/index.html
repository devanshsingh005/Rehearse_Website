1. Overview & Our Commitment

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

We are committed to handling your data responsibly. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Commitment We've written this policy in plain English to ensure you understand exactly how your information is handled.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is collected automatically:

  • Account information (Name, Email) received from Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In
  • Content you upload (Sheet music files, PDFs, or images)
  • Communications you send to our support team
  • Technical data (Device model, OS version, app usage logs)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Manage your account using Supabase Authentication
  • Store and retrieve your files using Supabase Storage
  • Process your uploaded sheet music to generate playable audio
  • Respond to support requests and send service updates
  • Improve app performance and detect security issues

4. Third-Party Data Processors

We use trusted third-party providers to operate Re-Hearse:

  • Supabase: Secured backend for database, storage, and authentication
  • Apple and Google: Authentication infrastructure
Privacy First We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active. You may delete individual files at any time.

Account Deletion: You can delete your account via App Settings. Upon deletion, all associated project data and profile information will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at devansh.singh20045@gmail.com.

7. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures (including encryption in transit and at rest via Supabase) to protect your data.

However, as no method of transmission is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Re-Hearse is not intended for children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes & Contact

We may update this policy periodically. Your continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
